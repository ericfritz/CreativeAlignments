Elizabeth Morris

Senior Recruiter

Elizabeth is a highly experienced recruiter and human resources consultant with more than 15 years of experience in the industry. She brings a broad range of expertise to Creative Alignments in recruiting, staff management, operations, benefits assessment, training, and organizational development. Elizabeth has worked in a wide variety of industries including life sciences, finance, technology, and advertising.

Outside of the office Elizabeth enjoys hiking, skiing, cooking, and being a mom. She holds her Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certification and coaching certification from the Coach Training Institute. Elizabeth also has completed 360-Degree Recruiting Life Cycle training from Next Level Recruiting Training. She received her Bachelor of Science degree in human ecology from the University of Wisconsin.
