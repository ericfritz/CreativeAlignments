R U Ramping Up Now?

If your company is in rapid growth mode, we can fill your open reqs with great hires quickly while freeing up your time and saving you money.
With most of our R.U.N. clients, we will place 1 to 3 recruiters onsite at your offices to make the process of hiring a high volume of new employees both efficient and effective. We can blend seamlessly into your team, working closely with your hiring managers, HR director and/or others. We can be as strategically-focused (establishing recruiting processes, improving your employment brand, etc.) or as execution-oriented (simply filling reqs) as you desire us to be. We can increase or decrease our team’s weekly hours according to your ever-changing business needs. While you benefit from the state-of-the-art sourcing tools we have invested in such as LinkedIn Recruiter seats, Gild subscriptions, DICE databases and more, we can fully integrate our process with any current business or applicant tracking systems you already have in place with your hiring process. Essentially, we are highly flexible and can adapt to your needs, processes, and styles accordingly.
