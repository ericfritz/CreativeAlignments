About Us

Creative Alignments is a trusted partner and go-to resource to fully manage the recruiting and placement of new
hires and provide ongoing human resources support and insight.
We know your space.
We work with businesses of all sizes, on projects large and small, with a special focus on Colorado’s growth industries such as high tech (software/IT); creative services (marketing/ design); and LOHAS (natural foods, products, and services).
We are nimble.
Our experienced team capably augments internal recruiting and HR departments, either from our clients’ offices or from our headquarters in downtown Boulder.
We ease your workload.
We don’t simply forward stacks of resumes hoping one is the right fit, then move on. Instead, we work closely with our clients to attract, hire, and keep the best talent.
An engagement with us might look like this:
  • We ensure that we clearly understand our client’s business and recruiting needs.
  • We write clear, targeted job descriptions.
  • We search for candidates in a highly strategic manner, using a full array of search tools and our extensive networks.
  • We creatively find talent and reach outside the box to source candidates who may not otherwise have applied for the job.
  • We screen and interview potential candidates prior to introductions to the client.
  • We keep track of all candidate activity and share the activity report with our clients throughout the process.
  • We coordinate client-side interviews and feedback to candidates.
  • We check candidate references and facilitate background checks.
  • We help write and present offer letters and coordinate an overall on-boarding plan.
  • We provide ongoing consulting expertise and support as desired.
