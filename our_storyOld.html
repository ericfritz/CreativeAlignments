Our Story

Creative Alignments is a recruiting agency and human resources consultancy like no other. Through our innovative pricing model and attentive, personalized, “everyone wins” approach, we help find the right hires for our clients while saving them time and money.

Intentionally stepping away from the traditional contingency model of recruiting, which can be impersonal, costly, and difficult to manage, we developed a simple, smooth, cost-effective way for clients to approach their hiring needs. We use an hourly billing model so our clients pay just for what they need, when they need it.

Whether our clients have many positions to fill or are keeping busy juggling ordinary growth, we represent them with integrity throughout the hiring process. As a nimble hiring resource, we can work from our clients' offices during a vigorous hiring phase, seamlessly integrating into the fabric of their culture, or we can work off-site from our headquarters in downtown Boulder, Colorado. We also provide consulting expertise and support through our a la carte offerings of HR services.
