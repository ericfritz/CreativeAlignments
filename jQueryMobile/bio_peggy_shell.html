Peggy Shell

Founder

Peggy founded Creative Alignments in 2010 to provide an excellent alternative to the traditional contingency model of recruiting, which can be impersonal and costly. Peggy and her team bring a high-touch, innovative approach to clients through an hourly billing model, which saves our clients time and money. Peggy began her career at Tesser, a nationally recognized brand and design agency based in San Francisco, where she was the HR Director from 1999-2003. Since 2003, Peggy has worked as an independent consultant, helping her clients as a go-to resource to fully manage the recruiting and the placement of new hires, and providing ongoing support and insight for various HR needs.

When Peggy isn’t busy building her business, she enjoys being a mom to her two boys and is a regular yoga devotee. Peggy received her Bachelor of Science degree in mathematics and education from Northwestern University.
