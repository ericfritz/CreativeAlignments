Lee Mebel

Senior Recruiter

Lee joins Creative Alignments with over 15 years of recruiting, human resources, and financial experience. He began his consulting career in corporate finance with Accenture and a Denver-based Cognos partner before moving into the external recruiting field. This led him to RE/MAX where he managed the recruiting and HR functions for six years before returning most recently to the HR/recruiting consulting field with Creative Alignments. Lee has worked extensively with technology, financial, accounting, and sales recruiting for various industries.

Lee holds a professional human resources certificate (PHR) and a Bachelor of Arts degree in business and geography from the University of Mary Washington. You'll find him volunteering with inner-city youth, climbing, kayaking, and running the mountains of Colorado in his spare time.
