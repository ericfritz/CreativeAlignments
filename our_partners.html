Our Alliances

It takes a village to be successful. We value our relationships with the many individuals and organizations that collaborate with us as we deliver the best for our clients. Here are some of our favorite partners:

Luke's Circle

Luke’s Circle is a network and email list of talented professionals seeking opportunities in Boulder-Denver growth companies. It connects some of the area’s leading employers to a circle of qualified professionals and also provides those professionals with direct access to the latest local opportunities.

In March 2013 Luke’s Circle and Creative Alignments launched a partnership to offer a valuable new service for job-seeking professionals. This is a rare opportunity to receive personalized advice directly from a professional recruiter on how you can improve your job search prospects. This one-on-one phone session will cover some of the following topics:
  • What employers and recruiters look for and how they review candidates
  • Strategies for how to improve your job search
  • High-level resume feedback
  • Optimizing your LinkedIn profile and the importance of social/online profiles done well
  • Resources for finding opportunities
  • Networking suggestions
  • Tips for pursuing a job search remotely (if applicable)
You can learn more about and/or sign up for your own personalized Job Search Strategy Session at www.lukescircle.com

Red Idea Partners

Red Idea provides strategic consulting for promising growth companies and entrepreneurs. Our "Venture Works" component partners with select companies. We help clients scale their businesses realistically and intelligently.

Our consulting abilities span many aspects of business: core strategy, finance, fundraising, sales and marketing, brand development, operations and team hiring/development. We are senior specialists and advisors from many facets of business and industry, bringing "best practice" teams and solutions to address our clients’ needs. www.redideapartners.com

Relish Creative

Relish Creative is a design + strategy studio servicing clients with branding, packaging, and web design needs. We aim to bring your brand to life in innovative, dynamic ways that can communicate through many different touch points.

Relish Creative is headed by Elysia Syriac. Her fresh, modern outlook toward design brings her clients’ ideas to life in an extraordinary way. Her research skills provide in-depth, relative solutions and experience with print design and interactive allows for a unified vision across all mediums.
