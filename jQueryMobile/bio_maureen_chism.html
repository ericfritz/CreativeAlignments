Maureen Chism

Senior Recruiter

Maureen brings more than 14 years of recruiting, sales, client services, and training experience to Creative Alignments. Her passion and innovative attitude, along with her excellent people and interviewing skills, instill confidence in candidates and clients alike. Prior to joining Creative Alignments, Maureen spent 13 years at Highland Technical Staffing where she co-led the recruiting efforts and helped successfully grow the business. She works extensively to deploy, train, document, support, and recruit technology candidates for various industries.

In her free time, Maureen enjoys playing and coaching volleyball, biking, hiking, skiing, volunteering, and spending time with her family. Maureen holds a Bachelor of Science degree in elementary education from Eastern Michigan University.
