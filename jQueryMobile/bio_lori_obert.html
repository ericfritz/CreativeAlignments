Lori Obert

Senior Recruiter

Lori brings a unique blend of skills to Creative Alignments. She recruited primarily in the pharmaceutical industry for Management Recruiters International and as a sales specialist for Johnson & Johnson, where she assisted with the recruiting, hiring, and training of new sales representatives. Lori also has a strong writing background, having worked for Channel 9 News as a writer and producer. At Creative Alignments, she has conducted searches for a variety of roles including IT related positions, finance & accounting, and sales positions for natural food companies.

Lori has a Bachelor of Science Degree in journalism with honors from the University of Colorado and a Master of Arts degree in International Communication from American University.
