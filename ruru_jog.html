R U Juggling Ordinary Growth?

If your company is looking to hire 1, 2, 5, or perhaps even 10 new people this year, we can be your go-to recruiting resource.
Some of our J.O.G. clients choose to outsource their complete hiring process to us, while others draw on our expertise and networks for specific aspects of the process. Perhaps you would like to have us expand your applicant pool by skillfully sourcing passive candidates. Or perhaps you are flooded with resumes and would like our experts to sift and sort and find the gems. Or maybe you'd like us to conduct phone screens and initial interviews.

A J.O.G. engagement with us might look like this:

  • We ensure that we clearly understand your business and recruiting needs.
  • We write clear, targeted job descriptions.
  • We search for candidates in a highly strategic manner, using a full array of search tools and our extensive networks.
  • We creatively find talent and reach outside the box to source candidates who may not otherwise have applied for the job.
  • We screen and interview potential candidates prior to introduction to you -- our client.
  • We keep track of all candidate activity and share the activity report with our clients throughout the process.
  • We coordinate client-side interviews and feedback to candidates.
  • We check candidate references and facilitate background checks.
  • We help write and present offer letters and coordinate an overall on-boarding plan.
  • We provide ongoing consulting expertise and support as desired.
