We
don’t simply forward stacks of resumes, hope one is a fit and
leave the rest up to you. Rather, given our expertise in the broader realm of human resources, Creative Alignments works closely with our clients throughout the hiring and retention process.
An engagement with us might look like this: We first clearly understand the client’s business and needs underlying any new hire. We help strategically write an appropriate job description, search in a highly targeted manner, screen and interview potential candidates prior to introductions to the client, coordinate client-side interviews and feedback to candidates, perform reference checks, help write and present offer letters, facilitate plans for an initial start date and overall on-boarding plan, and provide ongoing support as needed for other human resources needs.
